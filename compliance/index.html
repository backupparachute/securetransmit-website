Compliance

SecureTransmit gives you the primitives to build compliant B2B data-exchange workflows. We’re not advertising certifications we haven’t earned.

What we build with

  • Immutable, hash-verified audit trails retained separately from file data
  • AES-256 at rest, TLS in transit
  • Configurable retention windows, including delete-on-ack (Paranoid mode)
  • Sealed mode for pre-encrypted payloads we move but never decrypt
  • Island mode for outbound-only architectures
  • Least-privilege access and credential-scoped firewall rules

Certifications

We are not advertising SOC 2, HIPAA, or other certifications today. When we’ve completed audits we’ll list them here with the audit firm and date, not before. If you need to know where we are on a specific framework for your procurement review, reach out and ask.

Working with regulated workloads

If you’re evaluating SecureTransmit for a workload that has to meet GDPR, HIPAA, PCI DSS, or another framework, we’re happy to walk through the architectural controls with you and be honest about what we can and can’t support today.
